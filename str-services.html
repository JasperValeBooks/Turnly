Core service levels

Choose the baseline, then add only what you need.

Standard Turn

Guest-ready clean + staging + reset of critical touchpoints.

  • Floors, trash, dusting, kitchens & bathrooms.
  • High-touch disinfection.
  • Bed reset (linen swap if provided).

Deep Clean

Periodic refresh for better reviews and long-term maintenance.

  • Baseboards, fan dusting, cabinet/oven detail.
  • Behind/under key appliances (where accessible).
  • Extra time, scheduled when it fits turnover timing.

Laundry & Linen Options

Offsite laundering workflows + optional linen solutions to simplify turns.

  • Professional wash/dry/fold approach.
  • Optional linen program (if you offer it).
  • Keeps cleaners focused on cleaning.

Restock management

Always stocked, always ready. Track and replenish consumables based on your chosen tier.

Essentials

  • Toilet paper, paper towels, soap, trash bags, pods.
  • Basic counts customized to your property.

Business

  • Everything in Essentials plus coffee/tea, tissues, spices, etc.
  • Inventory notes + reorder triggers.

Custom

  • Build your own list: welcome baskets, local gifts, special touches.
  • We follow your brand standards.
